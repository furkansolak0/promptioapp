Account Deletion Request

This page explains how to delete your Promptio account and associated data.
Support: help@promptioapp.com Age 13+

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open Promptio
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

If you can’t access the app

Email us from the address linked to your Promptio account:

Email account deletion request
What gets deleted: Your account email and any account-associated data (e.g., favorites and unlock status) are deleted or de-identified where feasible.

What may be retained: Records required for legal, tax, or compliance purposes (for example, purchase history) may be retained for the minimum period required by law.

Timeframe: We typically process requests within a reasonable time.
