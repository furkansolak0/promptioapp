1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Promptio (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Promptio mobile application. By using Promptio, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information depending on how you use the app:

  • Account information: email address and a user identifier to create and manage your account.
  • App activity: prompts you view, favorite, and unlock (via premium or rewarded ads), and basic in-app interactions.
  • Purchase status: information indicating premium entitlement (we do not receive or store your payment card details).
  • Device & diagnostics: device model, OS version, language/region, and crash/performance diagnostics.
  • Advertising data: advertising identifier (such as Advertising ID on Android) and limited device information used for ad delivery and measurement.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as precise GPS location, contacts, photos, or health data).

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide core features (browse prompts, favorites, unlocks).

  • Verify premium access and restore purchases.
  • Operate rewarded ads to unlock content.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and prevent abuse/fraud.

4. Ads, Advertising ID & Third-Party Services

Promptio uses rewarded ads provided by third-party ad networks (for example, Google AdMob). These providers may collect and process information such as the device’s advertising identifier, IP address, approximate location (e.g., country/region), and ad interaction data for ad delivery, measurement, frequency capping, and fraud prevention.

Depending on your device settings, location, and consent choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized as permitted by applicable law and platform policies.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share information in limited cases:

  • Ad providers: for rewarded ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.
  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, and crash reporting (only to operate and improve the app).
  • Legal & safety: if required to comply with law or to protect users, our rights, or prevent fraud/abuse.

We do not sell your personal information.
